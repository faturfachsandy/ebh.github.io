Energi Batubara Hitam

Established in 2007 located in the region Samarinda, East Kalimantan, Indonesia. We are focused to produce and selling steam coal worldwide.

Energi Batubara Hitam is and Indonesian incorporated company that enggages in coal mining and sales both domestic and international customers from varius industrial sectors.

From our head office in Samarinda, East Borneo Province and our representative officer in Jakarta, Paser, Balikpapan, Banjarmasin, Jambi, and Bengkulu, Energi Batubara Hitam cooperate in a large mining area. Supported by professional employes and personnel from contractors and associated companies, Energi Batubara Hitam sales target production capacity is 750.000 tonnes per year.
